SYSTEM_PROTOCOL_FAQ_v3.0

COMMON
QUERIES.

SEC 01

To ensure fair competition, there are two separate competition tracks: Group 1 for FY (First Year) students and Group 2 for SY, TY, and BTech students. Each group has its own leaderboard and prize pool.

SEC 02

CodeSprint (Solo) is a 2-hour high-intensity challenge. Pair Programming (Duo) is a collaborative 3-hour session. Both tracks focus strictly on DSA and Problem Solving.

SEC 03

The competition is open to all MITAOE students across all academic years and branches. We encourage everyone interested in problem-solving to step in.

SEC 04

This is a strictly OFFLINE championship hosted at the MITAOE Campus. We believe the energy of an in-person arena is unmatched for learning and networking.

SEC 05

The prize pool is ₹35K+. Both FY and Senior groups have dedicated cash prizes. Shortlisted participants from the Pair Programming track also get exclusive opportunities for internship interviews (₹25K+) and placement kits.

SEC 06

Yes! You can test your solo skills in CodeSprint and team up for Pair Programming. Separate registrations are required for each track.

SEC 07

Yes, all participants who compete in the arena will receive an official certificate of participation from CodeChef Club MITAOE.
